How ZPLUS Works

ZPLUS Water Softener is installed inside the water storage tank. It contains a food-grade material that reacts with Calcium (Ca) and Magnesium (Mg), changing their conditions to neutral. This prevents them from reacting with air and forming salt or scaling.

  • Breaks down heavy particles in water
  • Protects Taps, Tiles, RO, Geysers, etc.
  • Easy 5-minute installation
  • No electricity required
  • Portable and easy to move

Easy Setup

Installation Process

ZPLUS Water Softener is designed for a hassle-free, 5-minute installation.

  • 1 Location: Install inside your water storage tank.
  • 2 Position: Hang it vertically using a rope.
  • 3 Height: Keep it 1-2 inches above the bottom of the tank.
  • 4 Flexibility: Portable and easy to remove if not in use for long periods.
Verify Results

Water Quality Check

You can verify the effectiveness of ZPLUS Water Softener in just 5 minutes with a simple boil test.

Step 1: Collect

Take equal volumes of raw water and ZPLUS treated water in separate utensils.

Step 2: Boil

Boil both samples for the same amount of time and let them cool down.

Step 3: Observe

Raw water will appear turbid with salt layers. ZPLUS water will remain crystal clear.
