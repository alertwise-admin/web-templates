How ZPLUS Works

ZPLUS Water Softener is installed inside the water storage tank. It contains a food-grade material that reacts with Calcium (Ca) and Magnesium (Mg), changing their conditions to neutral. This prevents them from reacting with air and forming salt or scaling.

  • Breaks down heavy particles in water
  • Protects Taps, Tiles, RO, Geysers, etc.
  • Easy 5-minute installation
  • No electricity required
  • Portable and easy to move
