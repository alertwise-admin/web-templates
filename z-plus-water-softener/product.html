How It Works

ZPLUS Water Softener contains a specialized media that interacts with the hard water minerals (Calcium and Magnesium). Instead of removing them (which requires salt and regeneration), it structures them in a way that they lose their ability to stick to surfaces. This process is known as sequestration or conditioning.

Benefits

For Your Home: Prevents white scaling on taps, tiles, and bathroom fittings. Keeps your kitchen and bathroom looking new.

For Your Appliances: Scale buildup is the #1 killer of water-using appliances. ZPLUS prevents this buildup in geysers, washing machines, dishwashers, and RO purifiers, significantly extending their lifespan and efficiency.

For You: Hard water can cause dry skin and hair fall. By neutralizing the hardness, ZPLUS helps in maintaining the natural moisture of your skin and hair.

Installation Steps:

  1. Open your overhead water storage tank.
  2. Take the ZPLUS Water Softener unit.
  3. Tie it with a nylon rope (provided or use your own).
  4. Lower it into the tank until it is about 1-2 inches above the bottom of the tank.
  5. Secure the rope at the top of the tank.
  6. That's it! The system is now installed and working.

Note: For best results, clean the unit with normal water every 2-3 months to remove any sediment that might have settled on it.
