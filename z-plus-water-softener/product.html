The Ultimate Hard Water Solution

ZPLUS Water Softener is a cutting-edge, non-electric water conditioning system designed to protect your home and family from the damaging effects of hard water. Unlike traditional softeners that require salt, electricity, and frequent maintenance, ZPLUS uses a unique sequestration technology to neutralize hard water minerals effectively.

How It Works

Hard water contains high levels of Calcium and Magnesium ions, which bond together to form stubborn limescale deposits on surfaces. ZPLUS contains a specialized food-grade media that interacts with these minerals at a molecular level. It modifies their structure so they lose their adhesive properties. As a result, the minerals remain in the water (keeping it healthy to drink) but cannot stick to your pipes, taps, appliances, or skin.

Easy 5-Minute Installation

ZPLUS Water Softener is designed for a hassle-free DIY installation. You don't need a plumber or any special tools. Just follow these simple steps:

1
Open Water Tank

Go to your roof and open the lid of your overhead water storage tank.

2
Prepare the Unit

Take the ZPLUS Water Softener unit and tie it securely with a nylon rope (usually provided or available at hardware stores).

3
Positioning

Gently lower the unit into the water tank. Ensure it is suspended about 1-2 inches above the bottom of the tank. It should not touch the floor of the tank to ensure proper water circulation.

4
Secure & Close

Tie the other end of the rope securely to the top of the tank or the lid handle. Close the tank lid.

Important Note

For optimal performance, we recommend cleaning the unit every 2-3 months. Simply pull it out, wash it with normal water to remove any sediment or mud that may have settled on the surface, and put it back in.
